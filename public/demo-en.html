❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is this only for forex brokers?

No. Tradesite Autopilot works for any trading-related business — forex, crypto, CFDs, prop firms, signal providers, education companies, and affiliate marketers in finance.

Can I customize the generated content?

Absolutely. All AI-generated content — website pages, blog posts, social media, emails — is fully editable. Modify, rewrite, or regenerate any piece at any time.

How does the infrastructure layer work?

We pre-purchase aged domains, build blog networks, secure press placements, and acquire social media accounts. When you create a project, these assets activate for your brand — giving you instant authority.

What happens after the 90-day plan?

The 90-day timeline is your launch roadmap. After that, all systems continue — daily posting, blogs, emails, communities. Extend or modify the strategy anytime.

Do I need technical skills?

Zero. The platform handles everything — domain setup, website deployment, SEO, social scheduling. If you can click a button, you can build a brand.

Can I export my website?

Yes. Export your complete website as a ZIP with all HTML, CSS, and assets. Deploy anywhere — your hosting, Netlify, Vercel, or any server.
